# Hand-gesture-particles--updated
The updated version of hand gesture particles which is sensitive to two hands at the same time and can control the brightness aswell. this version consists of more complex shapes than the previous version.The Spiral and a Torus are the two shapes i selected for this version which needs more depth and shadow details for better user experience.
Spiral is one of my favorite shapes because of its symbolism of the Fibonacci sequence aka golden ratio and the weird sense of artzy it beholds.However the shape in the particle system is not exactly the golden ratio but rather an evenly spaced spiral which honestly cancels the whole idea of fibonacci sequence. Just wanted a reason to add my favorite shape. <img width="1152" height="2048" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2570c703-1275-4825-b760-ed4a2bd04e5f" /> <img width="1152" height="2048" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3ba66e8c-5f05-4806-958f-b8eed668a426" />
The next shape being the torus, which is different but yet another interesting shape with lots of curvature.So creating it takes time but really fun.
the verticle palm movement decides the shape; palm in the upper part of the screen is for spiral shape, when the palm is moved vertically downwards shape converts to a torus. 
pinching of index finger and thumb decides the size and the number of fingers raised in the second hand decides the brightness of the particles.
