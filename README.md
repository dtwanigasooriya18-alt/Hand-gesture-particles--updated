# Hand-gesture-particles--updated
The updated version of hand gesture particles which is sensitive to both hands at the same time and can control the brightness aswell. this version consists of more complex shapes than the previous version.The Spiral and the Torus are the two shapes i selected for this version which needs more depth and shadow details for better user experience.
Spiral is one of my favorite shapes because of its symbolism of the Fibonacci sequence aka golden ratio and the weird sense of artzy it beholds.However the shape in the particle system is not exactly the golden ratio but rather an evenly spaced spiral which honestly cancels the whole idea of fibonacci sequence.( Just wanted a reason to add my favorite shape.) <img width="1152" height="2048" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2570c703-1275-4825-b760-ed4a2bd04e5f" /> 
The next shape is the torus, which is another interesting shape with lots of curvature to work with.
The verticle palm movement decides the shape; when the palm is moved to the upper half of the screen it creates a spiral shape and when the palm is moved vertically downwards shape converts to a torus. 
pinching of index finger and thumb decides the size while the number of fingers raised in the second hand decides the brightness of the particles.
